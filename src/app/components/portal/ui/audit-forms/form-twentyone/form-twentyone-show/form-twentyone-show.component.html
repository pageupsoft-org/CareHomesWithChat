Workplace Inspection

Provide a score between 0 and 3 for each question on the inspection list, depending on the current situation. As shown below on the marking scale 0 would be scored when there is nothing in place. A partial compliance would give a score of 1 or 2 depending on the degree that a requirement has been met. A score of 3 would show that a high level of health and safety standards is in place. Where a score is 0 to 2 then comments on the current situation and future remedial actions must be written in the comments box or in an additional report. Some questions may not be applicable to all services. Please provide any comments that might relate to the improvement of the Service Health and Safety resources and support.
